Privacy Policy

Effective date, September 13, 2025

Who we are

Smart Cart Savings provides research driven guidance that helps shoppers save on grocery delivery. Contact us at hello@smartcartsavings.com or by mail at Sample business address, 123 Market Street, Suite 200, San Francisco, CA 94105.

Information we collect

  • Contact information when you send us a message, for example name, email, and the contents of your request.
  • Usage information from standard web server logs, for example pages viewed and general device data. We use this to improve content quality.
  • Optional analytics if enabled in the future. We will update this policy before enabling analytics and will provide a simple opt out.

How we use information

  • To respond to your messages and support requests.
  • To maintain site security and reliability.
  • To improve articles and navigation based on aggregate trends.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not publish private messages or email addresses.

Cookies

This site uses essential cookies for basic functionality. If we add analytics or preference cookies later, we will list them here and provide clear choices.

Data retention

We keep contact emails as long as needed to handle your request, then archive or delete messages on a rolling schedule. Server logs rotate automatically on a short cycle.

Your choices

  • You can request a copy or deletion of your contact messages by emailing hello@smartcartsavings.com.
  • You can disable non essential cookies when offered, and you can clear cookies in your browser.

Children

Our content is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use standard security practices appropriate for a small publisher, including limited access to mailboxes and basic server hardening.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page when our practices change. Material updates will include a note at the top of the page with a new effective date.

Contact

Email hello@smartcartsavings.com for privacy questions or requests. You can also write to the postal address listed above.
